HELP OUTA PAW

This is a shelter for you who loves animals. Here you are able to spend time with cats and dogs of every breed in need. Whether you like acrobatic cats or chronically lazy dogs, there will be a perfect friend here waiting for you.

Our only law is to help out a paw!

HOAP is not a common animal shelter! You can do a lot here, scroll down to find out.

What can you do?

Spend your time with us and gain new four-legged friends

You can come down to our shelter and have some fun time with our cats and dogs. You can also pick a specific time in our calendar to cuddle a cat or walk a dog in the nature.

Help us run errands and make the animals feel at home

You can help us with running errands. Get notified when we need some help, whether it is picking up a huge cat tree or saving some animals which got abandoned and need you to pick them up.

Make sure no stomachs are empty by donating to us

By donating money, you will make it easier for us to keep every stomach in the shelter full, and you can count on us spoiling them with toys and treats.
